About Gain Town

About the Project

What is the group project?

Our group project is a fitness-focused website called GAIN TOWN that provides users with personalized workout plans, resource links, and if it was a fully fledged idea would provide subscriptions for exclusive content and access to personal trainers.

What inspired the site?

We were inspired by the rise in home fitness trends and the lack of reliable online exercise resources. We also saw a lot of existing sites that were stuck behind a paywall.

What is the site’s purpose?

The purpose of the site is to provide a user-friendly platform for individuals to build and manage their own workout routines and access curated resources.

Who is the intended audience?

Our goal is to attract primarily people who are new to fitness; however, the site should also appeal to those who wish to spice up their workout routines.

What does the site do?

The site uses user inputs and a database to find related resources. with further development the site will use traffic to promote popular programs and resources to users based on activity.

How does it fulfill the audience’s needs?

The site provides a more seamless way for people to get into exercise by offering a hub of resources and tools. we use a database of resources to easily provide the user exercise content they want to gain from.

How and why is this site relevant today?

Exercise never goes out of fashion, our site helps connect people to exercise for free without the shame and paywalls, and from the comfort of your home.
